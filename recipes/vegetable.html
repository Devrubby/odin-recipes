Vegetable Soup

Description

The Nigeria vegetable soup is a popular delicacy in Nigeria.

It is commonly served during occassions of importance

Also it is very delicious and very nutriotion, and consumed by almost all enthnic groups around the country

Although it can be very expensive and, difficult to prepare.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 kg of beef
  • 1 kg of dried fish
  • 1 cup of lobster (dried)
  • 2 bunches of water leaf
  • 2 bunches of vegetable leaf
  • 5 bell pepper
  • 1 cup of shrimp
  • 1 cup of palm oil
  • A cup of avocado oil
  • Onion
  • Salt and blended Pepper
  • Maggi

Steps

  1. Soak the dried fish in hot water for four hours
  2. Wash and cut the beef into bite size pieces
  3. Transfer the beef into a pot
  4. Add a little water, chopped onions, maggi, and salt to taste
  5. Cover the pot and set it on fire. Allow it to boil for about 40 minutes
  6. Wash the vegetable on a dish
  7. Chop the washed vegetable leaf into thin slice with a knife
  8. Pour the avocado oil into a frying pot, that is sets on a fire
  9. Add the onions. Let them fry until they are translucent.
  10. Add the blended pepper to the onions and allow the paste to cook for about 20 minutes
  11. Drain the water from the fish
  12. Add the boild beef from step 5, fish, shrimp, lobster, pepper, and onion
  13. Stir them together and allow to cook for about 15 minutes
  14. Add the vegetable leaf
  15. Add the palm oil
  16. Stir together and allow to cook for about 10 minutes
  17. Your delicious meal is ready to be served
